Introducing myRA A simple, safe, affordable account to kickstart saving for your future. Brought to you be the United States Treasury
Sign up to be the first to know when myRA is available.

What are the benefits?

Saving should not cost you money or heartaches

  • No fees. Ever.
  • No minimum to open.
  • Flexibility to contribute as much as you want: $50, $25, $7—any amount!
  • You can withdraw from the principal without penalty whenever you need it (for example, to cover medical bills).

Available wherever you work, even if you are self-employed

  • Your plan stays with you even when you change jobs.
  • If you have more than one job, you can contribute from all your paychecks into a single myRA account.

Easy to start and keep saving

  • If your employer is making myRA available, they can setup automatic deposit out of your paycheck. So if you choose to save $50 per pay period, they will deposit $50 into your myRA instead of your bank account.
  • If your employer is not making myRA available, or if you are self-employed, you can still set up an automatic deposit from your bank account into your myRA account.

A safe investment with steady returns

  • Investment is backed by the U.S. Treasury
  • Your investment never loses value
  • Once you save $15,000 (or save for 30 years), you can then roll your myRA over into a private Roth IRA.
