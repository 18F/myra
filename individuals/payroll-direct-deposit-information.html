Payroll Direct Deposit Information

Currently, you can fund your myRA account through any employer as long as the employer offers direct deposit and is able to direct a portion of your paycheck to your myRA account.

In the future, the United States Treasury will be making other methods available for you to contribute to your myRA account.

You may or may not qualify to contribute to a myRA account at this time.

A myRA is a Roth IRA, and like other IRAs, requires that you (or your spouse, if married filing jointly) have taxable compensation to be eligible to contribute. For specific information on your eligibility to contribute to an IRA, you may want to speak to a financial advisor.
