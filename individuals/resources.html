myRA Resources for Individuals

We’ve created resources that can help you learn more about myRA and get you through the signup process. Those resources include:.

  1. Direct Deposit Instructions - This is a letter you can give to your employer when you hand in your direct deposit authorization form. Download Direct Deposit Instructions
  2. Direct Deposit Authorization Form – Complete this form and give it to your employer to set up automatic payroll contributions into your myRA account. Download Direct Deposit Form
  3. Brochure - A brochure on why myRA is a great way to start saving for retirement. Download Brochure
  4. Infographic - This is a one page visual summary of what myRA is all about. Download Infographic
  5. Top Questions About myRA – Some frequently asked questions about myRA for your reference. myRA that can be printed or emailed. Download Top Questions About myRA
  6. Welcome to myRA – You have opened a myRA account, now what? This letter outlines the next steps.
  7. Key Roth IRA Features – This document summarizes the key features of Roth IRAs. Because myRA is a Roth IRA, it follows Roth IRA rules. myRA that can be printed or emailed. View page.

We appreciate your feedback. Please let us know if you have thoughts on how we can improve these resources by emailing myra@treasury.gov.
