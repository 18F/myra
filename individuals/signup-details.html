Thank you for your interest in saving with myRA

It is possible that you may not qualify to contribute to a myRA at this time.

A myRA is a Roth IRA, and like other IRAs, requires that you (or your spouse, if married filing jointly) have taxable compensation to be eligible to contribute. Under the IRS compensation rules, being unemployed may keep you from contributing to a myRA. For specific information on your eligibility to contribute to an IRA, you may need to speak to a financial advisor.
