Signup Details

Currently, you can only fund your myRA account by direct deposit through an employer, especially through an employer that participates in the myRA program. However, you can fund your myRA account through any employer as long as they offer direct deposit and they are able to set up a portion to be directed to your myRA account. In the near future, the U.S. Treasury will be making other methods available for you to contribute to your myRA account.
