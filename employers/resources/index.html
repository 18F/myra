Resources for Employers

Make doing the right thing easy. There’s a no-fee, simple way to help your employees start saving for retirement with myRA.

Simply set up a meeting, email your employees and provide printed and digital information in your workplace.

We’ve created tools you can use to help your employees learn about myRA and get signed up.

Meeting-in-a-Box

Everything you need to set up a meeting with your employees

  1. Emails - Modify these emails to invite your employees to a meeting.
  2. PowerPoint Presentation - Brief, easy-to-digest presentation to use in the meeting.
  3. Talking Points - Key facts to reference as you talk with employees.
  4. myRA information for your website - This text can be used on an internal web page devoted to retirement plan options or other employee information. It can also be modified for use in an internal newsletter.
