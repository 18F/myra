myRA Resources for Employers

Helping your employees start saving for retirement with myRA is easy.

We’ve created tools you can use to help them learn about myRA and get signed up.

Choose the tools that work best for you

  1. Guide for Employers - A guide to help participating employers introduce myRA to their employees. Download Guide for Employers
  2. Posters - A larger piece to hang in the workplace, intended to grab employees’ attention. Download Green Poster | Download Blue Poster
  3. Brochure - A double-sided brochure that can be printed or emailed. Download Brochure
  4. Infographic - Helps explain myRA in simple, straightforward language. Can be used online or printed. Download Infographic
  5. Web Banner - Directs people to myRA.treasury.gov. For use on a company intranet site. Download Web Banner
  6. Top Questions About myRA- Frequently asked questions about myRA that can be printed or emailed. Download Top Questions About myRA
  7. Intranet Content - If you have an internal company website (intranet) for employees, you can post this information about myRA. Download Intranet Content
  8. Employee Email on myRA - Use this to explain myRA to employees and encourage them to sign up. Download Employee Email on myRA
  9. Employee Meeting Toolkit - Use these resources to set up a meeting with employees about myRA.
    • Employee Invitations and Follow-up Emails - Use these emails to invite employees to meetings on myRA and to follow up with them after the meetings to reinforce the myRA messages. Download Employee Invitations and Follow-up Emails
    • PowerPoint Presentation - Use this brief PowerPoint presentation in meetings to explain the features and benefits of myRA and how to sign up. Download PowerPoint Presentation
    • Key Fact Sheet About myRA - Use these facts along with the PowerPoint presentation or separately as a way to answer questions about myRA. Download Key Fact Sheet About myRA

You will need an application like Adobe Reader to view PDFs. Get Adobe Reader
