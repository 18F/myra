myRA is a brand new way to help your employees save for retirement. It’s simple, safe and affordable—for your employees and you.

Easy for your employes. Even easier for you.

No hassle for employers

  • No paperwork for employers: There's not forms you need to fill out or program to enroll in. All you do is set up direct deposit for your employees' paycheck.
  • No employer contribution: myRA is funded entirely by employees' contributions.
  • myRA is not an ERISA plan:

Employees love it

If you have employees who aren’t eligible for your employer-sponsored retirement plan, or if you don’t offer a retirement plan, myRA is a way to help your employees build healthy financial habits and save for their future.

Learn more about what it's like for individuals »

A new way to save

myRA is not a replacement for traditional retirement plans. Instead, it's targeted for employees who are U.S. citizens and who do not have access to employer-sponsored plans. American companies and nonprofits of any size can help their employees signup.

How it works

Simple for savers, even simpler for employers

1. Talk to your employees

Share information about myRA with eligible employees. Information can be downloaded here.

2. Employees create their accounts

Interested employees visit myra.treasury.gov to create their account.

3. Setup direct deposit

After creating their account, employees will receive a form to complete, which they will bring to you to set up their direct deposit. Once you’ve set up their direct deposit, there’s nothing else you need to do.
