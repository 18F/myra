Benefits of myRA.

myRA is a brand new retirement account that's easy for individuals—and even easier for employers.

No hassle for employers

There are no forms for employers to fill out or program to enroll in. All you do is set up direct deposit for your employees' paycheck. There is no employer contribution; myRA is funded entirely by employees' contributions. And myRA is not an ERISA plan.

Employees love it

If you have employees who aren’t eligible for your employer-sponsored retirement plan, or if you don’t offer a retirement plan, myRA is a way to help your employees build healthy financial habits and save for their future.

Learn more about what it's like for individuals »

A new way to save

myRA is not a replacement for traditional retirement plans. Instead, it's targeted for employees who do not have access to employer-sponsored plans. American companies and nonprofits of any size can help their employees signup.

How it works

1. Talk to your employees

Share information about myRA with your eligible employees. Click here to download materials.

2. Employees create accounts

Interested employees visit myRA.treasury.gov to create their account.

3. Setup direct deposit

After creating their account, employees will receive a form to complete which they will bring to you to set up their direct deposit. Once you’ve set up their payroll direct deposit, there’s nothing else you need to do.
