Benefits of myRA

What it is

myRA is a new retirement savings account being developed by the U.S. Treasury designed to help individuals without access to employer-sponsored retirement plans save for their future.

myRA will be easy for employers to make available to their employees. There will be no employer contribution, match or account.

What it's not

  • myRA is not another stack of paper work for you to fill out.
  • myRA is not an ERISA plan.
  • myRA is not an auto IRA.
  • myRA is not a replacement for traditional employer-sponsored retirement plans.
