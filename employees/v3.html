Introducing myRA:

A simple, safe, affordable account to kickstart saving for your future.

Brought to you be the United States Treasury

Enter your email address to be the first to know when myRA is available.

Who myRA is for:

U.S. citizens looking for a new way to begin saving for retirement.
People without access to an employer sponsored retirement plan.
Individuals earning less than $129,000 a year (individuals) or $191,000 a year (couples).

Benefits of myRA

Saving should not cost you money or heartaches

  • Never any bank fees.
  • No minimum to open.
  • Flexibilty to contribute as much as you want: $50, $25, $7—any amount!
  • Life sometimes gets complicated. You can withdraw from the principal without penalty if you need to (medical bills, etc).

Available wherever you work, even if you are self-employed

  • Your plan stays with you even when you change jobs

Easy to start and keep saving

  • Automatic payroll deposit into your myRA if your employer is a partner.
  • Automatic bank deposit into your myRA if your employer isn't a partner, or if you are self-employed.

An safe investment you can trust

  • Investment is backed by the full faith and credit of the U.S. government.
  • Your investment never loses value
  • Once you save $15,000 (or save for 30 years), you can then roll your myRA over into a private Roth IRA.
