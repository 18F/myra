Introducing myRA:

A simple, safe, affordable account to kickstart saving for your future.

Brought to you be the United States Treasury

Enter your email address to be the first to know when myRA is available

Who myRA is for:

People without access to an employer sponsored retirement plan
People with savings between $x,000 and $x,000
Individuals earning less than $129,000 a year

Benefits of myRA

Saving should not cost you money or heartaches

  • Never any bank fees.
  • Increase or decrease how much you save anytime you want. Start with as little as $5 a month. But start!
  • Life sometimes gets complicated. You can withdraw from the principle without penalty if you need to (medical bills, etc).

Available wherever you work, even if you are self-employed

  • Your plan stays with you even when you change jobs

Easy to start and keep saving

  • Automatic payroll deposit into your myRA if your employer offers a plan
  • Automatic bank deposit into your myRA if your employer doesnt’ offer the plan, or if you are self-employed.
  • At anytime you can manage how much you want to save each pay period.

Save more money than a savings account, but be just as safe

  • Investment is secured by the US Treasury similar to a savings account.
  • Your investment never loses value
  • myRA interest rate is [x] times the average interest rate on a savings account.
  • Once you save $15,000 (or save for 30 years), you can then roll your myRA over into a private retirement account—and earn an even higher interest rate.
