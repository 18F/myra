• The myRA story

    Learn how myRA works through the life of Olivia.

  • Meet Olivia

    She just graduated from college and started a new job.

    So she opened an myRA.

    Her employer is already a myRA partner, so she just asks them to put $25 every month into her account.

  • And then Olivia moved and got a new job.

    She tells her new employer to keep depositing into her account.

  • Olivia gets a big promotion.

    She bumps her monthly savings up to $100 and now her myRA is really growing.

  • Olivia has some unexpected hospital bills.

    She withdraws $3,000 from her myRA tax-free and penalty-free and she lowers her monthly contribution to $25 for a few months.

  • After 15 years she’s reached $15,000!

    Olivia can’t save any more money in her myRA but she can now roll it over to a Roth IRA.
