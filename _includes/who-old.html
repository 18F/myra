Who should use myRA?

    • You should set up a myRA if…
    • You want to start saving for retirement, and
    • You don’t have access to an employer-sponsored retirement plan (a traditional 401k), and
    • You earn less than $129,000 per year (less than $191,000 per year if you’re a married couple filing jointly)
  • As a part-time worker, I don’t have a retirement plan through work, so myRA is perfect for me. Olivia, 27, sales associate
