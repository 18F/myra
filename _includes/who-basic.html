Is myRA right for me?

  • You should set up a myRA if…
  • You want to start saving for retirement
  • You're a wage earner without access to a retirement plan at work and
  • You earn less than $129,000 per year (less than $191,000 per year if you’re a married couple filing jointly)

Currently, you can only fund your myRA account by direct deposit through an employer.
