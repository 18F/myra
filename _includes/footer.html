myRA: My Retirement Account

myRA is a retirement savings account for individuals looking for a simple, safe, and affordable way to save for their retirement. There will be no fees to open and maintain a myRA and the account will never lose value. The investment will be backed by the U.S. Department of the Treasury. Soon, individuals will be able to open myRA accounts and begin contributing to them every payday. myRA will be initially offered through participating employers.

Questions?
