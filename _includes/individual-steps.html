How does it work?

myRA has been designed for you. Each pay period, you’ll be able to automatically deposit money from your paycheck into a myRA account. This money will grow risk-free, earning interest. You can save up to $15,000 this way.

Here's how it works:

  • 1. Create an account
  • 2. Set up regular savings
  • 3. Watch your investment grow
  • 4. Take the next step

1.Sign up for myRA

First, you will open your myRA account by providing your information online or by phone — it will take about 10 minutes.

Click here to get started

2.Set up regular contributions from your paycheck

Give a direct deposit authorization form to your employer. You'll have the option to print one when you open your account, or use the one you receive in the mail with your welcome package. If you want to contribute from multiple jobs, give each employer a form.

If your employer does not currently participate in the myRA program, but does offer direct deposit, we have a letter that explains what they need to do to get your direct deposit set up.

Download direct deposit instructions | Download direct deposit form

3.Sit back, relax and watch your savings grow

Because of your automatic contributions, all you have to do is watch your savings grow. myRA contributions earn interest at the same rate as the government securities fund for federal employees. You’ll be able to check your balance and manage your account online 24/7.

If you already enrolled in myRA Online, click here to manage your account.

4.Reach your goal

You can keep saving money in your myRA for 30 years or until you’ve saved $15,000. You can roll over your myRA into a private-sector individual retirement account at any time.
