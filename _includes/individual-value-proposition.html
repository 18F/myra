Why is myRA a good option?

Saving is now easier than ever. Whether you can regularly save $7, $25, $50, or $100 a month, myRA is a simple and reliable way to kick start saving for your future.

Simple

  • Set an automatic deposit every paycheck
  • No penalties for withdrawing your contributions
  • Withdraw earnings tax-free after age 59½ (if your account has been open at least five years)
  • The account is not tied to your employer. It stays with you if you change jobs and you can use it from multiple jobs

Safe

  • myRA will never go down in value
  • Your investment is backed by the U.S. Treasury
  • Your account is private and secure

Affordable

  • You pay no fees
  • It costs you nothing to open an account 
  • You choose what to contribute each payday ($2, $20, $200—whatever fits your budget!)* 
  • You also get all the tax advantages of a Roth IRA
