Why is myRA a good option?

Saving is now easier than ever. Whether you can regularly save $7, $25, $50, or $100 a month, myRA is a simple and reliable way to kick start saving for your future.

Simple

  • Set an automatic deposit every paycheck
  • No penalties for withdrawing contributions in an emergency
  • Withdraw earnings tax-free at age 59½ (if your account has been open at least five years)
  • Portable – take it with you if you change jobs; use it from multiple jobs

Safe

  • Savings will never go down in value
  • Your investment is backed by the U.S. Treasury
  • Your account is private and secure

Affordable

  • No fees and no cost to open an account
  • Savings will not go down in value
  • Contribute as much or as little as you want every payday ($50, $25, $7 – any amount up to $5,500 per year!)
  • Get all the tax advantages of a Roth IRA
